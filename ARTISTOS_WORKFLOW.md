# ArtistOS — Complete Workflow

## The Faceless Artist Management Platform

---

## THE 8-STEP WORKFLOW

```
1. ARTIST PROFILE     → Who is the artist?
2. SONG CONCEPT       → What is the song about?
3. SONG STYLE         → How does it sound?
4. WRITE LYRICS       → The words
5. VISUALS/ANIMATION  → Cover art + video
6. DISTRIBUTION       → Post to platforms
7. FAN REQUESTS       → Collect + analyze comments
8. RELEASE CALENDAR   → Schedule + plan drops
```

---

## STEP 1: ARTIST PROFILE
**"Who is this artist?"**

- Artist name + brand identity
- Genre(s) — what kind of music?
- Target audience — who listens?
- Influences — who do they sound like?
- Persona — what's their story?
- Visual style — faceless = silhouette, abstract, etc.

*This is the FOUNDATION. Everything flows from who the artist is.*

---

## STEP 2: SONG CONCEPT
**"What is this song about?"**

- Vibe/mood? (hype, chill, emotional, party)
- Topic? (love, street life, celebration, struggle)
- Story we want to tell?
- Target listener?

---

## STEP 3: SONG STYLE
**"How does it sound?"**

- BPM range
- Key
- Genre
- Reference artists
- Instrumentation (808s, synths, hi-hats, etc.)

---

## STEP 4: WRITE LYRICS
**"What are the words?"**

- Intro (0:00-0:15)
- Hook (0:15-0:45)
- Verse 1 (0:45-1:20)
- Hook Repeat (1:20-1:50)
- Verse 2 (1:50-2:25)
- Bridge (2:25-2:40)
- Verse 3 (2:40-3:05)
- Outro (3:05-3:15)

---

## STEP 5: VISUALS & ANIMATION
**"What does it look like?"**

For faceless artists:
- Abstract/silhouette avatar (never shows face)
- Animated cover art (waveform, floating elements)
- Genre-specific visual style
- YouTube thumbnail + 3-5 min video

---

## STEP 6: DISTRIBUTION
**"Where does it go?"**

| Platform | Content Type | Goal |
|----------|-------------|------|
| YouTube | Full song video (3-5 min) | Ad revenue, virality |
| YouTube Shorts | 30-60 sec clip | Algorithm reach |
| TikTok | 30 sec teaser | Viral moment |
| Instagram | Reel with lyrics overlay | Fan engagement |
| Spotify | Full song | Streaming revenue |
| Apple Music | Full song | Streaming revenue |

**Release order:**
- Day 1: YouTube (full) + Spotify + Apple Music
- Day 2: TikTok clip
- Day 3: Instagram Reel
- Week 2: YouTube Shorts (lyric snippet)

---

## STEP 7: FAN REQUESTS
**"What does the audience want?"**

**The Engagement Flywheel:**
```
Listeners comment requests → AI analyzes top requests → 
Song generated based on demand → Release → Fans share → 
More listeners → More comments → Repeat
```

**How it works:**
1. Post a teaser: "Comment what BR CHIEF should rap about next 👇"
2. Collect comments for 48 hours
3. AI analyzes: most requested topic wins
4. Generate song using that topic
5. Credit the fan who suggested it
6. Release + notify: "Your idea dropped — listen here 👇"

---

## STEP 8: RELEASE CALENDAR
**"When does everything drop?"**

**Why it matters:**
- Controls release pace (don't drop everything at once)
- Times content to events (graduations, holidays, summer, etc.)
- Queues fan-requested songs
- Builds anticipation with consistent schedule

**Example Calendar:**

```
MAY — "BAYOU BANGER SEASON" Pre-Release
├── Week 1: TIGER CITY (lead single)
├── Week 2: STEPPIN TO ME (fan-favorite)
└── Week 3: BAYOU BANGER (cookout anthem)

JUNE — Album Launch Month
├── Jun 1:  INTRO (album opener)
├── Jun 7:  INTERMISSION (transition)
├── Jun 14: NO BOUNCE (club banger)
├── Jun 21: CRUISIN (summer drive)
├── Jun 28: DREAMWORLD (experimental closer)
└── Jun 30: Full Album "BAYOU BANGER SEASON" drops

ONGOING — Fan Request Cycle
└── Every 2 weeks: Fan-request winner released
```
