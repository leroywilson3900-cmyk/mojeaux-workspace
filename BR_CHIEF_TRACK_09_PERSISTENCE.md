# 🎤 BR CHIEF — PERSISTENCE
## "The One That Got Away" / "Still Here"

---

## 📋 SONG SPEC

**Duration:** 3:25-3:50
**Vibe:** Emotional, reflective, raw truth — smooth flow, not hype
**Mood:** Dark to light — struggle to triumph
**Key Feature:** Transitions from slow reflective verses to a harder, resolved hook

**NO ZYDECO.** Just raw emotion and a beat that breathes.

---

## 🎵 STYLE PROMPT (Instrumental)

```
Slow, brooding 808s fading in — minimal piano chords, soft hi-hats, 
southern trap drums that build. 100-110 BPM, D minor, emotional rap beat. 
No zydeco. No hype. Deep, introspective Southern hip hop. 
Think: P

CeeLo "The One That Got Away" energy meets J. Cole "Love Your 
Yourz" vulnerability. Full mix builds from sparse to full by hook.
```

---

## 🎤 LYRICS PROMPT (Vocal)

```
Reflective, vulnerable rap — deep voice, smooth delivery, no yelling. 
39-year-old perspective, three daughters, 22 years with his wife. 
Perseverance through racial struggle, being unseen, working twice as hard. 
Not a complaint — a declaration. Honest, raw, full of meaning. 
Louisiana-born resilience. Emotional depth, not shock value.
```
