# 🎤 BR CHIEF — BEST AI RAPPER
## "The Inheritance"

---

## 📋 SONG SPEC

**Duration:** 3:30-3:45
**Vibe:** Orchestral hip-hop, braggadocio, lyrical showcase
**Mood:** Confident, powerful, melodic but hard-hitting
**Key Feature:** Choir + violin + guitar orchestration. NO techno, NO 808s dominant. Live instrumentation feel.

**This is BR CHIEF channeling Lil Wayne energy — intricate wordplay, punchlines, metaphors — but with orchestral soul behind him.

## 🎵 STYLE PROMPT (Instrumental)

```
Hip-hop beat with LIVE ORCHESTRAL ARRANGEMENT — gospel choir humming the hook, 
symphony strings (violins, cellos) layered with clean acoustic guitar plucks. 
Hard-hitting snare, minimal 808s (subtle, under the live instruments). 
110-120 BPM, C minor, epic cinematic feel. 
Think: Kanye West "Power" meets Lil Wayne's intricate flow over live instrumentation. 
NO techno. NO synths dominating. Choir + strings + guitar = the foundation. 
Rap delivery: rapid-fire, intricate rhyme schemes, punchline-heavy.
```
