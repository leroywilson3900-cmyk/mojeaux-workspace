# 🎤 BR CHIEF — LAST SONG (JAM ON IT 2.0)
## "The Final Countdown"

---

## 📋 SONG SPEC

**Duration:** 3:30-3:50
**Vibe:** 80s electro-funk meets Southern marching band — nostalgic but fresh
**Mood:** Party urgency, last call energy, "get your dance on before the lights come on"
**Key Feature:** Marching band tubas + 80s synths + Southern University band energy + rawNSa drum pattern

**BR CHIEF IS NOT MENTIONED A THOUSAND TIMES.** Let the music speak.

---

## 🎵 STYLE PROMPT (Instrumental)

```
80s ELECTRO-FUNK meets SOUTHERN MARCHING BAND fusion.
RawNSa drum pattern (that classic "jam on it" beat) — hard kick, rhythmic hi-hats.
Tuba bass lines from a Southern marching band — fat, deep, marching groove.
80s analog synthesizers — poly synths, Prophet-style pads, vocoder hints.
Symphonic brass stabs — short, punchy, marching band style.
140-150 BPM — fast enough to march, funky enough to dance.
Think: Newcleus "Jam on It" meets Southern University marching band energy.
NO trap drums. NO 808s dominant. Live brass + synth bass = the foundation.
Keep it modern — crisp production, not lo-fi.
```

---

## 🎤 LYRICS PROMPT (Vocal)

```
BR CHIEF versatile rap flow — this is NOT a club banger, it's an 80s funk jam.
Smooth delivery, party vibe, fun energy, not aggressive.
Song about: the DJ is about to play the LAST SONG — lights about to come on.
Everyone better get their dance on, shake what their momma gave em, 
because the night is almost over.
80s electro funk vibes, not trap. No mention of BR CHIEF more than once or twice — max.
Confident but fun, not braggadocio. Everyone wins this one.
```
