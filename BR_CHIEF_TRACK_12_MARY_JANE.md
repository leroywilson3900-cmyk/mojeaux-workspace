# 🎤 BR CHIEF — MARY JANE 2.0
## "The Love of My Life"

---

## 📋 SONG SPEC

**Duration:** 3:30-4:00
**Vibe:** Classic funk, celebratory — Rick James "Mary Jane" energy, WEED IS THE WOMAN
**Mood:** Laid back, celebratory, love song to marijuana, funk groove
**Key Feature:** METAPHOR throughout — Mary Jane is the weed AND the woman. Every punchline hits hard.

**PUNCHLINES & SLANG REQUIREMENTS:**
- Dense wordplay, metaphors throughout
- Every line should have something clever
- Southern/Louisiana slang + weed culture terminology
- CHOPPED & SCREWED terminology: hipsters (acting crazy/high), choppers (frequent smokers), screwed up, slowed down, faded, crossfaded, too gone, floatin', observin', etc.
- HOUSTON SLANG throughout — we don't never, don't try to, homie, fa sho, etc.

## 🎵 STYLE PROMPT (Instrumental)

```
CHOPPED AND SCREWED HOUSTON FUNK — 70-80 BPM, slow and laid back.
Deep 808 bass that hits like a heartbeat, crisp hi-hats with triplets.
Chopped and screwed effect — slowed down vocals, reverb soaked.
Funky live instrumentation — wah-guitar, synth pads, muted trumpet.
```
