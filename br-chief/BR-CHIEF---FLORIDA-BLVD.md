# 🏙️ BR CHIEF — "FLORIDA BLVD"

---

## THE CONCEPT

**Why This Song:**
Florida Boulevard is the main artery of Baton Rouge — the strip that runs through everything. It's not pretty. It's not what LSU shows on TV. But it's REAL. Every BR kid who grew up on the Northside knows Florida Blvd. The cookouts. The cars. The cruising. The late nights. The grind. Florida Blvd is a state of mind.

BR Chief grew up on it. He's from it. And this song is his love letter to the street that made him.

**The Vibe:**
Slowed, chopped-and-screwed. Melodic. Nostalgic. Drake meets Paul Wall meets BR. 85 BPM — feels like riding down Florida at 2AM with the windows down and the system bumping.

**The Mood:**
Nostalgic. Proud. Street. Melancholic but confident. This is the song that makes BR kids from Florida Blvd feel seen.

---

## SONG: "FLORIDA BLVD"

**Genre:** Chopped-and-Screwed BR Melodic Rap × Slowed

### MUST-HAVE ELEMENTS:
- 🎸 Chopped-and-screwed guitar sample (Houston DNA)
- 🎹 Minimal piano (one chord, looped)
- 🥁 Slow 808s (not too heavy — keep it smooth)
- 🗺️ Ambient BR street noise in background (optional)
- 🎤 Vocal pitched down 5-10% (classic chopped-and-screwed effect)
