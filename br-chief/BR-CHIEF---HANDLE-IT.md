# 🔥 BR CHIEF — "HANDLE IT"

---

## THE CONCEPT

**Why This Song:**
This is the anthem. "Handle It" is BR Chief's mission statement. It's what he says to every person who doubts him, every city that sleeps on BR, every obstacle in his way. You handle your business. No excuses. No complaining. No backing down.

**The Vibe:**
Hard. Aggressive. Trap drums. Grungy. The kind of track that plays when you're getting dressed for work at 5AM and you need to lock in. Or when you're in the gym. Or when you're about to go handle something.

**The Mood:**
Grind. No mercy. BR Chief in full mode. This is not the club version — this is the 5AM "I'm about to go get it" version.
