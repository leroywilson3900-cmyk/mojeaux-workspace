## WHAT BR CHIEF IS

BR Chief is Baton Rouge's hypeman, ambassador, and chief. He makes HYPE DANCE music — the kind that makes you move whether you're in the club, in your car, or at home alone. His sound combines chopped-and-screwed roots (Houston DNA), Louisiana bounce, club-ready techno beats, grungy trap drums, and pure BR swagger.

He's not an LSU rapper. He's not a " Baton Rouge rapper trying to sound like Houston." He's a BATON ROUGE artist — full stop. His city is the star of every track.

**Key BR landmarks in his music:**
- 🏛️ State Capitol building (the capital of Louisiana — glowing purple and gold at night)
- 🌊 Mississippi River (running along the edge of BR)
- 🛤️ Florida Boulevard (the main strip, the heartbeat)
- 🌴 Bayous and cypress trees
- 🐯 Tiger Town (BR's identity — LSU is part of it, but tiger energy is the whole city)
- 🍜 Louisiana food culture (gumbo, jambalaya, crawfish)
- 🌙 Baton Rouge after dark (when the city really comes alive)
