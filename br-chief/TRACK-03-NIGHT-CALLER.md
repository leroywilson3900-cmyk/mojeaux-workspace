# 🌙 BR CHIEF — "NIGHT CALLER"

---

## THE CONCEPT

That track you play at 2AM when you're in the club and the DJ knows exactly what time it is. BR Chief is the Night Caller — he calls for the late night BR crowd. The ones who didn't go home when the club closed. The ones still riding. The ones who know BR at night is a completely different city.

**The Vibe:** High energy club anthem at 130 BPM. Not slow. Not reflective. Pure late-night hype. Drake's "One Dance" energy meets BR bounce meets 2AM DJ set.
