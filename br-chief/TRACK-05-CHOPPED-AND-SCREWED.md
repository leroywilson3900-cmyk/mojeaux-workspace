# 🔪 BR CHIEF — "CHOPPED & SCREWED"

---

## THE CONCEPT

This track is named after the legendary Houston chopped-and-screwed style — but in BR Chief's hands, it's turned UP. The chopped-and-screwed aesthetic (the slow, pitched-down sound) is used as the inspiration — but this is a HIGH ENERGY club version. Think DJ Michael "Chi" chopping it UP for the BR crowd.

**The Vibe:** High energy BR club banger. 128 BPM. References the chopped-and-screwed roots (Paul Wall, Mike Jones, DJ Michael "Chi") but delivered with maximum bounce and club energy.
